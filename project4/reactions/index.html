the Other Side

The problem is that I don't listen to the other side. I surround myself with people who share my beliefs. I was inspired by the Wall Street Journal's interactive that revealed just how divided our news intake have become. I created this project to expose us to multiple perspectives. If we take time to listen, maybe "they" won't seem so crazy!

by Ally Levine

A Map of Perspectives

Most of the liberal voices I've included are from D.C., New York City, and California, while those with conservative viewpoints come from all of the United States.

Thanks to the 2016 Election, I know I live in a bubble. My friends, family, and professors share identical views on most everything. The drawback is that I started to interpret opinion as truth, making it impossible to consider alternate viewpoints. My intention in this project is to offer a medium where we can see both sides at the same time.
